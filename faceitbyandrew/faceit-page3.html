Frequently Asked Questions

For group caricature sessions at parties, how long does each caricature take to do? Each basic caricature takes about 15 minutes to complete.

Can you send multiple caricature artists to larger parties such as weddings or bridal showers? Yes, we have several artists on hand who are able to come to your larger events. These should be booked well in advance.

What information is necessary to complete an order?

For event orders, we will need to know the date, time and location of the event. We will need to know the number of caricatures needed in order to know how many artists to send.
